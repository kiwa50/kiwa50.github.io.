Poem Collages I've made 2024-2026

Sourced from fashion magazines, bank statements, and miscellaneous ephemera I've picked up from nature walks. I cut snippets from interview and short phrases from film and book reviews. I incorporate different elements: dried flowers, dyeing with avocado pits, and infusing paper with essential oils. Melding storytelling with humor and the uncanny, the autobiographical and the mundance these poem collages, for me, are forms of play and meditation.
